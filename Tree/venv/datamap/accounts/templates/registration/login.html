Plants Database

The plants database contains plant entities in the region of Sofia, accompanied by relevant information about type of plant, exact location, health status, etc.

The plants included constitute either culturally significant plants that are inseparable part of the heritage of Sofia or plants that are subject to regular monitoring and maintenance.



Task Monitoring

The task monitoring presents an easy tool to create, track and assign plant maintenance tasks such as planting, fertilizing, trimming, watering, etc.

The immediate objective of the task monitoring is to save efforts and costs while maintaining a green and healthy environment in the city.
